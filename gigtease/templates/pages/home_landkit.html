How does GigTease work?

GigTease connects to your Spotify account and finds upcoming concerts near you based on bands you care about.

1

Connect

Authorize Gigtease to connect to your Spotify account. We will only use your account to create new playlists.

2

Preferences

Input the parameters including genre, time period, and distance from you that GigTease should use to build your new custom playlist.

3

Compile

Gigtease will find all the artists playing shows (based on your preferences) from SeatGeek and Ticketmaster's database.

4

Create

GigTease will generate a new Spotify playlist with the top song from each artist we found.

5

Listen

Either listen to your newly created playlist in GigTease or open up your Spotify app to discover new bands and concerts!

6

Showtime

If you dig any of the bands you hear, click the "Buy Ticket" button in GigTease to buy tickets to their show!
